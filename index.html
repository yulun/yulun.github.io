Yulun Zhang

Associate Professor
AI Institute, School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University
Email: yulun100@gmail.com

I have been a tenure-track associate professor at School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University since Mar. 2024. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Computer Vision Lab, ETH ZÃ¼rich, Switzerland. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Northeastern University, USA, in Aug. 2021. Before that I received my master degree from the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, China, in Jul. 2017 and B.E degree from School of Electronic Engineering, Xidian University, China, in Jul. 2013. I was an intern or visiting student at Adobe Research, Harvard University, Nanyang Technological University, The University of Sydney, and Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, CAS.

My research interest broadly includes machine learning and computer vision. Specifically, I focus on image/video restoration (e.g., super-resolution, denoising, deblurring), synthesis (e.g., style transfer, texture transfer), biomedical image enhancement and analysis, deep model compression (e.g., network pruning, quantization), computational imaging (e.g., spectral compressive imaging), multimodal learning, large language model, efficient diffusion model, etc. I am/was an Area Chair for CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML, ACM MM, IJCAI, AAAI, WACV, PRCV and Senior Program Committee (SPC) member for IJCAI, AAAI.

Our research has been generously supported by academic/industry sponsors, such as NSFC, Huawei, vivo, Lenovo, Adobe, MangoTV, Meituan, Transsion, and Tencent.
